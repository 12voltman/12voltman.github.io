Cards

I like designing space-optimized PCB business cards. Some are functional, some just decorative, but they all are fun little trinkets to have. If you have an idea for a business card PCB, or if you want to license or customize one of my designs, please reach out. I try to make my branding as unobtrusive as possible, and I'm happy to work with you to make a fully custom set with full commercial rights and branding. Here are some of the designs I've made so far:

  • Macro keypad (a la Stream Deck)
  • Mini game console
  • USB-C hub
  • Audio router for multi-monitor setups
  • NFC data bridge
  • USB trackpad
  • Phone-based environmental sensors over NFC
  • Internet uptime counter/ping chart
  • Audio visualizer
  • Midi drumpad
  • Animated emoji display
  • RISC-V development board
